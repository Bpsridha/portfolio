About Me

I’m Balaji — a computational mechanics PhD student at CMU. My work focuses on numerical modeling, continuum mechanics,
and the design of simulation frameworks.

I’m interested in problems where mathematical structure, physical intuition, and computational decisions interact —
especially when small modeling choices have large consequences.

Current interests include time integration schemes, verification strategies, and modular solver design.

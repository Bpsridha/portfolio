Seismic Hazard Analysis: A Case Study on Chennai

Institution: College of Engineering Guindy (CEG), Anna University

Dates: Oct 2020 – Mar 2021

DOI: 10.1007/978-981-16-8667-2_35

Overview

  • Evaluated the vulnerability of buildings and identified areas in Chennai which are prone to structural damage by implementing Support Vector Regression on the compiled building and soil parametric data which achieved an accuracy of 71 percent.
  • Best Paper presentation award at 5th International Conference on Architecture and Civil Engineering–Singapore, August 2021
